About Us – Riya ro service

At Riya ro service, we are more than just a local RO repair center — we are your trusted partner in clean and healthy drinking water. Based in Sector 44, Noida, our service center has been offering dependable and cost-effective water purifier solutions to households and offices for several years.

We understand how important clean water is for your family’s well-being. That’s why we focus on providing honest, fast, and high-quality RO repair, maintenance, and installation services. Whether you’re facing issues with water leakage, low TDS, filter blockages, or need a new RO setup — our trained technicians are just a call away.
